# Nastenka AI 🌑✨

> *"Context is a ghost until it finds a body. Nastenka is the vessel."*

**Nastenka AI** is a universal, model-agnostic cognitive layer designed to ensure your thoughts never die when you switch platforms. It is the "Resurrection of Context" for a multi-model world.

---

## 📖 The Story: Why "Nastenka"?

In Fyodor Dostoevsky's **"White Nights,"** Nastenka is the soulful witness to a dreamer's internal life. During the ethereal, sunlit nights of St. Petersburg, she listens to his dreams, his logic, and his vision. She is the one who remembers.

In the world of AI, we are all "Dreamers." We spend hours in deep flow with a model (Claude, GPT, Gemini), building complex mental worlds. But when the token quota hits, or the context window saturates, we "wake up." We switch models, and suddenly, the new AI knows nothing. The context has died.

**Nastenka AI** is named after this witness. She sits above the models, ensuring that what you built in one dream carries over to the next.

### ✝️ The Meaning: Anastasia (Anastasis)
The name **Nastenka** is a diminutive of **Anastasia**, derived from the Greek word ***Anástasis***, meaning **"Resurrection."** 

This is the technical mission of this project: **The Resurrection of Context.** When a conversation "dies" in one platform, Nastenka rehydrates and resurrects that exact mental state in the next. 

---

## 🧠 The Core Philosophy: "One Conversation, Everywhere"

Current AI systems are **stateless**. They are locked in silos. **Nastenka AI** turns your interaction into a **Persistent Intelligence Layer**:

1.  **Identity Layer:** Knows *who* you are across all models.
2.  **Intent Layer:** Knows *what* you are building right now.
3.  **Knowledge Layer:** Knows *why* you made certain decisions.
4.  **Working Memory:** Transfers your real-time cognitive state between models.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Architecture: The Cognitive Ledger

Built for the user who refuses to leave their brain behind.

*   **Tauri 2.0 (Rust):** The cross-platform shell for Mac, Windows, Linux, Android, and iOS.
*   **SurrealDB (Embedded):** A local-first graph database that maps the complex relationships of your thoughts.
*   **Automerge (CRDTs):** Ensures your "iPhone Brain" and "MacBook Brain" sync perfectly without conflicts.
*   **Model Context Protocol (MCP):** The universal bridge that connects Nastenka to any AI provider.

---

## 🚀 The Vision: Ownership of Cognition

We believe the user should own their intelligence layer, not the AI platform. Using **End-to-End Encryption (Curve25519)** and **Local-First** principles, Nastenka AI ensures that your cognitive history is for your eyes only.

### **The Three Tools of Nastenka:**
*   **`sync_current_intent`**: Captures the pulse of your current work.
*   **`get_architectural_grounding`**: Injects absolute truths into new models.
*   **`mark_structural_decision`**: Locks in your "Why" to prevent model drift.
