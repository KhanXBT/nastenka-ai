# Walkthrough: The Birth of Nastenka AI

This walkthrough summarizes the journey of defining **Nastenka AI**—a universal, user-owned cognitive layer designed for cross-platform intent and context persistence.

## 1. Defining the Problem (Context Fragmentation)
We started with a vision for high-fidelity context persistence. We identified that the core pain point is **Model Switching**—where a user moves from Claude to Gemini and loses "Invisible Intent," leading to hallucinations and re-explanation fatigue.

## 2. The Identity: Nastenka AI
The project was rebranded from "AI Alter Brain" to **Nastenka AI**. 
*   **The Vibe:** Soulful, personal, and constant.
*   **The Meaning:** "Nastenka" (Anastasia) signifies **"Resurrection,"** a perfect metaphor for context rehydration—where your thoughts "rise again" in the next AI model.

## 3. Refined Architectural Highlights
We designed a **Cognitive Ledger**:
*   **The Hybrid Memory:** Combining a **Knowledge Graph** (Long-term strategic rules) with a **Versioned State-Tree** (Short-term working memory).
*   **The MCP Bridge:** Using the industry-standard **Model Context Protocol** to ensure one brain can speak to any AI model (GPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok).
*   **Universal Presence:** A tech stack based on **Tauri 2.0** and **SurrealDB** (Embedded) to ensure the brain exists on MacOS, Windows, Linux, Android, and iOS.
*   **SS-Hybrid Sync:** A blend of **P2P (libp2p)** and **Private Relay** sync to ensure the "Brain never stays behind."
